Qualifications

While we will target low-income disadvantage students, all students are welcome to enroll and attend DTA classes. Acceptable age ranges are 18 years old and older. Students will need to submit their last school attended. The transcript will help identify the student's GPA. It will determine the student's efficiency in previous subjects, their actual level of educational proficiency and it wil be needed to take the State Test.

Accreditations

GED Testing Services (ged.com) is a nationally recognized and accredited curriculum for GED preparation and testing. They have graduated more than 2,000,000 individuals since their conception. DTA's partnership with GED Testing Services will help boost their numbers as well as ours.
